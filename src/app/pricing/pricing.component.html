Frequently Asked Questions

How do refunds work?

Refunds can be issued up to 48 hours before midnight on the day of your departure. For example, if your ride is on June 15, you are eligible to recieve a full refund until 11:59 PM on June 12.

Can I cancel my ride?

You may cancel your ride at any point, but refunds will be subject to our refund policy.

Where can I book a ride?

Rides can be booked at any time through the Hobo app, available on iOS and Android.

When does my ride depart?

All cars are scheduled to leave at 11:00 AM in the time zone of the departure city.

What payments do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express. No payment information is stored in our databases, and all payments are handled by Stripe. Stripe is the industry standard and is PCI compliant.
